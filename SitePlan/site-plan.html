Overview

Purpose

I am a website designer and a tailor. Does it bother you that many corporate businesses have their products made using child labor in other countries? I want to have a user-friendly website where I sell my handmade clothes made ethically in the USA. The website will be simple to navigate with a short purchasing process. The website will feature a category of products to choose from and a seach feature. Videos section. I would like to have a good photograph here and then the video link + their thumbnail if I can figure that out. I would like to showcase our products but I do not think that I can code the payment details. So I will just showcase what we have and say local pickup only. Perhaps an interview section would be nice. I will have to put in some work to do that however. Or maybe a "how to avoid looking like a kook" article would be funny and good. Team page. Photographs and social media links.

My intended audience are people who want to support ethically made items and also those in the scooter community.
